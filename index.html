Tonneau Covers

Product
Specs

Limitless Quality.
Limitless Potential.

Designed and manufactured with expert precision at our BC facility, our tonneau covers are a feat of engineering that can withstand up to 2000 lbs of weight. Tracks around 3 sides allow for multiple tie down points, and each cover is securely sealed to your box for safe and easy transport of cargo. It lifts up on gas-assisted shocks for easier unloading, and with a high-quality, durable aluminum frame and steel gussets, you can rest assured that your cover will be with you for the long haul. All installation hardware is included with every cover.
